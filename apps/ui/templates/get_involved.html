Get Involved

A3CP is being developed as an open, collaborative platform dedicated to communication as a human right. We invite care organizations, developers, researchers, and social-innovation partners to help shape an ethical, transparent, and freely replicable communication system for people who cannot speak. Your participation ensures that A3CP remains grounded in real-world care practice and guided by community values, not commercial interests.

For Care Organizations

We are building partnerships with welfare institutions, therapy centers, and special-education schools to co-design and test the first pilot version of A3CP. Partner organizations can try early prototypes, provide feedback from practice, and help refine adaptive interaction strategies that fit everyday care routines.

By joining the pilot network, you contribute directly to shaping a tool that will later be freely available for non-profit use worldwide.

Partner with Us

For Developers and Researchers

A3CP is fully open source and hosted under the GestureLabs GitHub organization. We welcome contributors in Python, FastAPI, Django, and machine-learning frameworks, as well as in user-experience and accessibility design.

Active development areas include:

  • Gesture and sound recognition
  • Adaptive memory and caregiver-feedback systems
  • Transparent audit logging and schema validation
  • Open data standards for multimodal communication research

All code, documentation, and issue tracking are maintained publicly to support open collaboration and long-term reproducibility.
